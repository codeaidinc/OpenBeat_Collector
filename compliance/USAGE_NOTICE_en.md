# Usage Notice (OpenBeat Collector)

Last updated: 2026-06-16

The tool is an aid for journalism/research efficiency that collects **public information only**. When using it, please observe the following.

## 1. Public information only

- Collection targets are limited to **publicly available** RSS feeds, article pages, etc.
- Do not attempt unauthorized access to authenticated, paid, or non-public areas. Where a paywall or similar prevents fetching, use the tool's manual-paste feature at the user's own discretion.

## 2. Summary + source-link principle

- The tool is designed to store headlines, summaries and source links; it is **not intended for redistribution of full text**.
- If you reuse or publish collected body text, you are responsible for fair-use/quotation limits, copyright, and proper source attribution.

## 3. Respect robots.txt and terms of use

- The tool **respects robots.txt** when collecting.
- **Compliance with each source's Terms of Use is the user's responsibility.** If a source's terms restrict automated retrieval or redistribution, do not register or collect from that source.

## 4. Personal-data care

- Public articles may contain personal data (names, affiliations, quotes, etc.). When collecting, storing, or using such data, comply with applicable data protection law (Japan APPI, EU/UK GDPR).

## 5. Responsibility division

- Provider (CODEAID LLC): platform safety, explainability, logs, incident response, security, and reasonable QA.
- User: input accuracy, source selection, compliance with terms of use, final decisions, and obtaining professional advice.
